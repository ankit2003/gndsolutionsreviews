Our Offerings

GND Solution is proficient in transforming concepts to solutions ranging from miniature to complex system designs. Our proven design practices ensure superior quality and on-time delivery first-time and every-time. Our design & development service offerings are.




gSense-100 [Soil Moisture Sensor]

    gSense-100 enable measurement of soil moisture levels by using frequency domain concepts. The sensor is made of a corrosion resistant element and giving it a long service life. gSense-100 is rugged, battery operated, low power & long-range sensor using IoT network.

gLite [Load ON/OFF Controller]

    gLite is a LoRaWAN™ compliant Load ON/OFF Controller. Its features upgrades lamp with intelligent, smart controlling and monitoring to any light or load equipment. gLite can be retrofit to any type of lights or load equipment’s.

GLoSe-916W [Preliminary Datasheet LoRa Water meter]

    GLoSe-916W is built on the LoRaWAN standard, it is ideal for low power, wide-area network (LPWAN). This product is designed to monitor Water flow rate.

gSense25 [Smart Waste Bin Sensor]

    gSensor30g is designed for waste bin level status detection. With LoRaWAN® module, it transmits the status via gateway to networks server and application server. User can monitor the status remotely.

gSense-30dl [Door Sensor ]

    gSense-30dl is LoRaWAN based door alert sensor. It sends immediate alert notifications on detecting door opening/ closing movements. The door open/ close duration and threshold limits are remotely configurable and can be programmed as per requirements.

gCard10 [Attendance/ Track]

    gCard10 is a small wireless device that broadcast signals using Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) technology.

gTag-10 [Beacon]

    gTag-10 is a small wireless device that broadcast signals using Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) technology.

GLoSe-916T [Temperature & Humidity Sensor]

    GLoSe-916T is a LoRaWAN™ compliant temperature and humidity sensor. The temperature and humidity data are two of the most basic information elements in cold chain management, smart factories, buildings & data center solutions.

gSense30AT [Asset Tracker ]

    LoRaWAN offers low-power transmissions of data over long range, with a flexible model allowing both public and private networks.

gSense130 [Cellular + Location Information]

    gSensor130 is a cellular based temperature sensor and powered by Li-Po rechargeable battery. It offers cost-effective sensor with Geo location information for a variety of applications.

gTrack-300 [Hybrid Asset Tracker]

    gTrack-300 is a hybrid asset tracker based on Cellular connectivity having GPS and BLE receiver. The asset tracker reads iBeacon and Eddystone beacon or customized Tag format and sends to local TCP server or internet HTTP/MQTT server.
